Among Them

Project Type: University Project
Software Used: Unity3D
Languages Used: C#
Roles: Programmer

About

Made for a Game Jam in University, Among Them is a find the murder-type game. With technological advances, society began to develop Androids, and forced these to work for them. After a group of Androids became aware of their reality, revolutions arose on their part against humans. Androids were then considered dangerous, and a group of detectives responsible for identifying them among the population was created.

Roles

Programmer

As the only Programmer, I was responsible for making all the scripts for the game. In only three days I programmed the gameplay, offline multiplayer, UI backend, etc.
